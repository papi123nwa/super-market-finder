Supermarket finder

This is a web application designed for locating the nearest supermarket around your location in Espoo. It shows the supermarkets belonging to S-group, K-group and Lidl.


Usage:
You can show your current location by clicking the icon in the left upper corner or type an address in the Find me -search.

You can also show the layers with 500 m and 1 km buffers around the supermarkets to check proximity.


Data source:
The data is made based on information from websites of the supermarket chains:
K-group, S-group, Lidl
